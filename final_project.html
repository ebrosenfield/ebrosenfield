Final Project: Apartment Pranks

For my final project, I am going to 'hack' our fruit bowl. With capacitor sensing and a small speaker, when someone touches the bowl, it will trigger the speaker to play Fruit Salad by the Wiggles.

My plan of attack

I am going to 'hack' our fruit bowl to play Fruit Salad when someone touches the metal bowl. To do this I will need to accomplish the following:

(1) Rig the fruit bowl with a capacitor sensor to trigger a response when someone touches the bowl.

(2) Get a mini speaker to play the song I want off of a Micro-SD card.

(3) Get the capacitor sensor and the mini speaker to work together so that one triggers the other.

(4) Obscure the electronics below the fruit bowl in a detachable module.

List of materials

  • Arduino
  • Micro-SD card adapter
  • 8-ohm speaker
  • Micro-SD card
  • Mini breadboard
  • BC 546b NPN transistor
  • 9V battery
  • Aluminum foil
  • Container/product to obscure electronics
